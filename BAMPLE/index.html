× HOW TO USE BAMPLE:

Load in any audio file up top (.wav, .mp3, etc)

Press the "BAMPLE" button (or tap the 'b' key) to begin pulling random samples from the sound file!

Use the slider to adjust the sample length.

Touch the pads or use the keyboard shortcuts to play your samples!

If you get a sample you like, drag it down into the saved sample pad section. Those bottom pads will stay locked even as you BAMPLE the top ones

Note: BAMPLE pulls randomly from across the whole audio file. There may be samples that turn up silent. That's okay! Just BAMPLE again to get new samples

Enjoy!
